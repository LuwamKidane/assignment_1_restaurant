Our famous food

Your local restaurant is known for its outstanding selection of great American food!

Our list of food is but not limited to...

  • Hamburger

Our hamburger is one of the greatest burgers of all time. Period. Nothing else needs to be said for how good it is!

  • Steak

Our steaks are made like steaks should be made. Medium rare is the longest we cook them. Every cut into the steak streams out the sweet-savory juice that makes them amazing!

  • Taco

Do you need something light to eat or are you in a hurry? Don't look farther than our tacos. You can add any toppings that we offer on your taco, making it one of the most popular items. Ask for a crunchy shell if you are feeling special or you can order it like a burrito.

  • Meatloaf

Meatloaf is the way to go if you aren't sure what you want to eat tonight. With it being a homemade special, there is not any sauce that needs to go with it. The meatloaf is just that good.

  • Beverages

We offer both alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. Our rich and smooth tasting wines come from a family-owned vineyard in Califorina.
